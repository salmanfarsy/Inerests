Interest in Anime

Anime changed the away i viewed cartoons.The anime was an opening to a whole new world . It wasnt that generic cartoons . it was much more.It really took story telling to another level.And its not just for kids.there contents for everyone.although I didnt even know i was watching anime without even knowing it.I came to realize later that a whole medium of exciting story telling was out there which was very good at delivering what was expected of it.

Death Note

I would say the world's best thriller not just anime but the best show ever made. a chase of cat and mouse. and also it makes you question what is true justice. i would highly recommend to watch it. as This will be the best anime for me.

Dragonball Z

My first action anime. with insane power ups. and out of this world fights . also with some of the most memorable characters. this is one of the classics of anime which inspired future animes and stories. it is one of the major show which made anime popular .

Fullmetal Achemist : Brotherhood

The world can be a dark place and everything is not what it seems to be. A story about two brothers and their journey to find answers and correct a mistake they made. which leads them to uncover even more secrets.

Naruto

A simple story of a outcast boy and his dream to be admired by all. but the story also is about modern ninja. which changed the view of ninja's forever.

Gintama

The best comedy show for me. although it has some vulgar scenes and dialouge but for me this is very funny . and for the record this is the best comedy anime of all time.and it also had a great storyline. overall its a great experience.

One punchman

You wanna see a hero struggling to be the best? well then this show is not for you. because it turn that concept on its head. the hero wins every fight without breaking a sweat. will there be a villain who could defeat hero.

My hero Academia

This is typical show about a boy without super powers becoming the best hero of the world. for the most parts its very enjoyable to watch the hero grow with other people around him. and also has some of the epic moments to be remembered. so fairly good enough.

Bleach

If i could delete the fillers this would have been a great show . but it was filled with too many fillers . still the first arc was the best part of the show . and also the captain of the thirteen squad are the main enjoyment for me.

Pokemon

Well i would say this is my first anime. and i didnt even know it was anime. a simple show for kids. i watched it like 600+ episodes. and i enjoyed most of it. although i cant say the same now.

Attack on Titan

The most epic anime ever made . this anime deserves its own genre and own theatre to be watched. its a masterpiece unlike anyother. the true brillence of a good story adapted perfectly for the short runtime.there is always more than what meets the eyes.
