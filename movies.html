Interest in movies

Movies I think I have wasted most of my life with this one word. And I think most people already well acquainted with movies. So I wont talk much about it just tell you about few of my favourite ones.

Bad Genius

Based on a true story. A movie about exam. Well i think its more than that Its about cheating in the exam. this movie is a simple master piece. Anyone can be hooked into this movie if they start watching. And this movie made exams thrilling as never before.its One of my all time favourite

One Cut Of Dead

Is this a horror movie or a comedy?. well judge it for yourself but this is one of the unique films made masterfully a long one take.and it also had a light weight story to make the film more compelling and well made. with much praise from the critics this is one of the Best for me

Dying to survive

Another movie based true story. This one really Is a character based story. Where you just dont enjoy the story but also get to see the character feel and develop in front of you. This movie is done in masterful away and One of my favourites of all time.

The Prestige

A story about magicians. But the Movie is Magic itself. An underrated Master class movie by Christophe nolan. This movie has a unique style of story telling with plenty of surprises. It can be said this Isn't just a movie. Its An entire magic show which also amazes the audience as if they really witnessed a misdirection of magic

Ode to my father

A life story of a man from childhood to his old age. The challenges he has to face at every stage of his life. The story is depicted beautifully. and It also has the happy and sad moments of life and makes it feel real with great acting.

Memoir of a Murderer

This is a story of a murderer. Who is chasing another serial killer. Do I need to explain anymore ? because the plot already seem interesting enough for me.But if you want more than its a story of a father with dementia and how he tries his best to protect his daught.

Miracle in cell no.7

A very emotional comedy movie about a mentally handicapped man and his daughter. This movie will make laugh cry and angry all the once. This movies knows what It's trying to do and does it very well. Keeping every aspect of the movie distinctive. This is a whole package and a Great story.

Pharang Pee mak

A horror story. May be or may be not. A great horror comedy with Great laughs and a light hearted story to make you feel good. Sometimes it might feel bit cringy but its still worth the laughs.

Midnight Runners

A story of two friends and Their one night encounter with a girl. Well not that kind of encounter but if you are thinking about girl getting kidnapped at night and finding her and rescue her from underworld Gangsters then you are right.

The Hobits

there are many movie franchise but I will choose this one because it has a dragon. Yes and Lord of the rings Didn't had dragon. So Yah thats about it. And its also more fun to watch except for the last one well Everything has its good and bad.
