Interest in Games

From my childhood I was fond of playing video games and specially Pc games.But I always had a low-end Pc so I played The games that ran on my pc So not many Triple A titles. And to be honest the soul purpose of getting a pc was to play video games So it was always one of my favourite hobby. But I Liked video games because You can see the result of your hard work in real time. When in the real world you always dont get the expected result of hard work. So video game was a great way to escape into another world. Although I was never really an expert in most of the games.

Assassin's Creed 2

This was my first open world game. So it was very memorable for me and only other game I completed twice other than sports games. And also I am gonna keep this as my one of the most favorite game of all time. The game play story and graphics everything was pretty good for its time. And it still lives on as the start of the most memorable character of assassins creed series Ezio.

Assassins Creed III

Well not my favorite or memorable as other assassins of the series. The story of Connor was some what exciting but more than the protagonist himself his father was more interesting character to me. And also, it’s a memorable game because it is the conclusions of the story of Desmond miles.

Assassins Creed: Rogue

The most interest part about this game was the exciting new character . the protagonist is not an assassin in this game but a templar so we finally get to play as a templar. The story is short and sweet. The game play is smooth and fun. Add another flavor the already big franchise of assassins creed.

COD: MW3

Very few shooting game has as exciting story as call of duty games. The entire modern warfare series was very fun and exciting to play. And there isn’t much to say as the name suggest the game is about the modern warfare.

COD: Black Ops

This game story wise is one of the best shooting game and one of my all time favorite shooting game. The game play is also great with some of the coolest graphics and epic moments which makes this game even more of a master piece. From the title screen to the end everything had a meaning and was exciting as any shooting could be.with a twist in the end to remember.

Battle Field 3

My very first shooting game. This game was simple and great too . with enough engaging storyline you can get invested in and also with good game play. But for me what stood out most was the graphics. It was very good for its time and well detailed making it look almost photo realistic. So the graphics of the game was very memorable for me.

WWE Raw Vs Smack down 2011

Well if you wanna just smash some button and have fun this the game. If you wanna enjoy slamming people this is the game. You and your friends wanna beat the heck out of each other than this is the game. For offline multiplayer and to play with others there is no better game than this. You can play with upto six player . and this game is memorable to me because I got have a lot of fun playing with my family and friends.

PES 19

If you want simple football game without too much technicality . then pes is the game for you. It is easy to play and with its career mode as manager and player is also very competitive and fun to play. I think if you wanna just have fun making your own team with your favorite player. this is a very good game for you. You can experience the game as you buy and sell player and make you team stronger.

Need for speed: Most Wanted

The best exciting racing game ever made with the best story line. It was may be made in 2005 but there is hardly another game that comes close to its unique story telling and engaging game play. With a good enough graphics and great game play this game is classic and must played.

Need for Speed Hot Pursuit

With photo realistic graphics and exotic cars. This game stands out as one of the best looking racing games. It may not have a story but the hot pursuits are exciting as it can be which it’s the best part of this game.
